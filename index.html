How it works

A simple loop that turns hesitation into momentum.

The product story is simple on purpose: notice the impulse, redirect the money, and keep seeing why those choices matter.

  1. 1

    Catch the urge

    When something tempts you, log the purchase you didn’t make while the decision is still fresh.

  2. 2

    Redirect the money

    Move that amount toward a goal that matters more than the impulse did.

  3. 3

    Build momentum

    Track progress, notice your habits, and let small wins stack into something meaningful.

Save for what matters, one skipped impulse at a time.

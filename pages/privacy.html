Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

NOPE ("we," "our," or "us") is a savings app that helps you reach your financial goals. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the NOPE app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information (when you sign in)

When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Profile photo (if provided by the provider)

Data You Create

  • Savings goals – amounts, descriptions, and completion status
  • Achievements – progress and unlock times
  • Preferences – currency (USD, EUR, UAH) and language (English, Spanish, Portuguese, Ukrainian)

Device & Usage Data

We may collect device identifiers and usage information to operate the app, fix issues, and improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the NOPE app
  • To sync your goals and achievements across devices when you sign in
  • To manage your premium subscription and trial
  • To send trial reminder notifications (if you enable them)
  • To respond to support requests

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services that may process your data:

  • Clerk – Authentication (Google, Apple Sign-In). See Clerk's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase – Cloud database for storing your goals and achievements when signed in. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat – Subscription and in-app purchase management. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Expo – App framework and push notifications. See Expo's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

Without sign-in: Your goals and data are stored locally on your device only.

With sign-in: Your data is backed up to our cloud (Supabase) and synced across your devices.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including secure storage for authentication tokens and encrypted connections.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. You can delete your account directly in the app through Settings. Deleting your account does not cancel your subscription. To cancel your subscription, please manage it through your device's App Store settings. For EU/UK users, we process your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

7. Children

NOPE is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date or through the app.

9. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at: support@saynope.app
